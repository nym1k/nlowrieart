naomi lowrie art

Hello! My name is Naomi Lowrie, and I'm a completely self taught painter based in Devon, UK.

Although I predominantly work with oil paints, I do love discovering and experimenting with different mediums. I draw inspiration from many sources – a colour, a place, a lyric… inspiration can be found anywhere! My favourite things to create are vividly colourful… and a little bit weird! I am a huge fan of the Surrealism art movement.

Below you’ll find a gallery of my original works – some of which are for sale and available as prints - over on my Facebook page and online store. My belief is that there is no limit to what you can imagine and my goal is to give the world a peek inside my imagination.

I hope you’ll like what you see!

- Naomi
